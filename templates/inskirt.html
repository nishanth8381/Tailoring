Measurements :
  • Length - 37
  • waist round - 38
  • seat - 48


Drafting (skirt part):
  • 0-1 and 2-3 = Length + 1 inch
  • 0-2 and 1-3 = Width + 1 inch

Drafting (seat belt):
  • 4" + 1"
  • 1/4 seat + 1"


Instructions


  • For body part small parts (2) have to attach to the big part
  • Make 2 pieces
  • Now two piece have to attach one side
  • Now upper part should measure and by using this measure next - belt part have to cut as length of the round and 5" width
  • Belt part have attach to the body part and fold towards the inside and now sides have to attach and down round should stitch.
