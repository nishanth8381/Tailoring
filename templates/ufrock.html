Umbrella Frock


Previous Next


Measurements :
  • full length - 38
  • chest - 36
  • shoulder - 14
  • waist length - 15
  • waist - 40
  • sleeve length - 5"
  • sleeve width - 13"

Drafting (Sleeve part) :
  • 0-1 = sleeve length + 1 inch
  • 0-2 = 1/12 chest
  • 2-3 = 1/6 chest + 2 + 1 inch
  • 1-4 = 1/2 sleeve round + 1 inch
  • x = mid of 0-3
  • x-x' = 3/4 inch
  • Deep the front arm hole


Drafting (Body part) :
  • 0-1 = waist length + 0.5 inch
  • 0-2 = 1/6 chest + 1 inch
  • 2-3 = 1/4 chest + 2 inches
  • 1-4 = 1/4 waist + 2 inches
  • 0-5 and 2-5' = 1/2 shoulder - 1 inch
  • x = midpoint of 5-5'
  • 0-6 = 1/12 chest - 0.5 inch
  • 0-7 = neck

Drafting (Skirt part) :
  • 0-1 and 0-2 and 0-3 = 1/6 waist
  • 1-6 and 2-5 and 3-4 = full length - waist length + 1 inch
  • x-x' = 1/2 inside (Deep the front at x')


Stitching methodology


Step 1 Step 2 Step 3 Step 4

Material should fold (2 layer) and mark to waist length

  • 0-1 = waist length
  • 0-2 = chest length
  • 0-6 = neck
  • 0-5 = shoulder
  • 2-3 = chest width
  • Now, back of neck have to cut to attach zip before attaching the zip, cloth have to fold and stitch. Now zip have to attach like overlapping to cover the zip
  • To the scalewidth, cross piece have to cut for neck piece. Have to stitch both back and front dots.
  • For neck [ back round - folding should keep to make round ]
  • For neck [ |_| shape - straight and big folding at corner to get shape ]
  • Even at the time of Stitching , cross piece have to cut to get shape ( |_| )
  • Attach the cross piece to the neck
  • Attach hands to the armhole side. for hands middle point have to mark and attach to the shoulder at mid point
  • After attaching hands, waist width have to measure
  • double to the waist width cloth have to cut in double fold
  • Now attach the cloth to the waist
  • Now connect the side parts to form frock
  • Down border have to stitch
