Pattiyala Pant


Previous Next


Measurements :
  • Length - 39
  • Seat - 48
  • Ankle - 13

Drafting (Bottom strip):
  • 8-9 = 1/2 ankle + 1"
  • 8-10 = 4"


Drafting (Belt):
  • 0-1 = 1/3 seat - 2"
  • 0-2 = 7+2 = 9"

Drafting (Bottom):
  • 0-1 and 2-3 = length - 7"
  • 0-2 and 1-3 = 1/2 ankle + 1"
  • 4-5 = length - 7"
  • 4-6 = material width
  • 6-7 = 7" to 8" | 1/3 seat - 5"


Instructions


  • 1. Have to cut centre piece
  • 2. Then side piece and belt
  • 3. Then bottom piece

Stitching Methodology


  • 1. Attach the belt ( 2 pieces ) with each other and stitch side corners
  • 2. Fold and stitch for 1" at the top for nada
  • 3. Now attach two side pieces to center piece
  • 4. Now attach bottom piece to the center piece
  • 5. Fold the center piece and stitch for stiffness
  • 6. Do same for the other side
  • 7. Now attach both legs at one side
  • 8. Attach legs middle to the belt middle
  • 9. Now attach remaining material with belt and putting foldings
  • 10. Now attach both legs
