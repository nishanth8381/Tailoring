Nightie



Measurements :
  • full length - 56
  • chest - 38
  • shoulder - 15
  • sleeve length - 6
  • sleeve round - 14

Drafting (sleeve part) :
  • 0-1 = sleeve length + 1"
  • 0-2 = 1/6 chest + 3"
  • 1-3 = 1/2 sleeve round + 1"
  • 0-4 and 2-5 = 1/12 chest


Drafting (Body part) :
  • 0-1 = full length + 1"
  • 0-2 = 1/6 chest + 1"
  • 2-3 = 1/4 chest + 2"
  • 1-4 = material width
  • 0-5 and 2-5' = 1/2 shoulder - 0.5"
  • 0-6 = 1/12 chest - 0.5"
  • 0-7 = back neck
  • 0-8 = front neck
  • x = midpoint of 5-5'
  • x-x' = 1.5" inside


Methodology



  • 1. After marking for shoulder and neck that is 1-5 1-6 1-2 and 2-3
  • 2. Now from 3 to 4 croosly have to mark till u
  • 3. Now for zip extra 3" width, 8" long piece have to cut and attach at the middle of the nightie from part/li>
  • 4. For that 3cm cloth , sides have to stitch 3 side and attach at the middle like (V)
  • 5. Now that cloth have to tuen inside by cutting at the middle
  • 6. Now that extra cloth have to turn inside and stitch
  • 7. Now zip have to attach
  • 8. shoulder have to attach
  • 9. Neck piece have to attach that ( crosspiece )
  • 10. hand have to attach
  • 11. sides have to attach
  • 12. now down have to stitch
  • 13. Flirts 2.5" have to cut equally
  • 14. For the Flirts round have to measure from shoulder to chest and cloth have to cut double to the measure
